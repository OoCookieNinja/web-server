SALADI YA MATUNDA-FRUIT SALAD

Ingredients

 2 large mangoes 4 bananas   

1 medium size papaya 

half pineapple 

2 oranges

 sugar (optional)

 METHOD 

Slice the oranges. peel and cut the , bananas,                                                        
pineapple and mangoes into fruit salad size cubes. Mix   
all the ingredients together.
